ABOUT ME 


                Faieq Idlan. For me to describe myself is quite a complicated task because I myself am not sure what I'm like to the people around me.
                I am just a simple boy roaming this earth meeting friends and creating memories along the way. Constantly learning and taking things 
                at my own pace.

                What I can say is there are a few words that I think can describe me;

                Loyal.
                And
                Hopeful.

                Loyal; To me, friends and family are the main reasons why I call myself loyal. I've spent five years of my life in MRSM Alor Gajah and there's 
                nothing I wouldn't do for them. We were there for each other every step of the way.

                Hopeful; In my opinion, I am the kind of person who always has hope. I learnt this through avid watching of animes. The characters taught
                me how I can never give up and lose hope on my goals. To always give your best and to try and try again. From the words of a famous 
                person whose name I can't seem to remember. 

                "There will always be light at the end of the tunnel" - someone
